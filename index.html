BMT Medical University

BMT(Buon Ma Thuot) Medical University is an oriented health science institution that has applied the first combined Private University-Hospital training model in the Central Highland region. This university has not only been providing high-quality medical education With modern medical equipment for the past decade with highly qualified international faculty but also has collaborated with 12 hospitals for clinical practice and placements.  BMTU University's commitment to top-notch education, cutting-edge medical facilities, and a holistic student experience makes it a distinguished choice for aspiring medical professionals in Vietnam

BMT University Hospital

This premier institution stands as the first and largest private hospital in the Central Highland region of Vietnam, our institution has been a beacon of compassionate healthcare for years. We pride ourselves on providing optimal healthcare services to our patients.

Our hospital integrates the functions of a university and a hospital, featuring specialized departments including Emergency, Surgery, Internal medicine, Cardiovascular Medicine, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Pediatrics, Laboratory, Diagnostic Imaging, Anesthesiology and intensive core, Infection Control, Pharmacy, Otorhinolaryngology, ophthalmology, dermatology, physiological, and Dietetics. This integrated approach ensures comprehensive care for our patients.
